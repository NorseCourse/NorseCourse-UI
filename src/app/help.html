Getting Started

NorseCourse is the new way to find your perfect schedule, created by students, for students.

Step 1: Find some courses

Start typing terms above to search for courses. You can search by course name, department, gen ed, or just plain words. Once you've found some you like, click the "plus" to add them to your schedule.

Step 2: Refine your preferences

Now you can head over to the "Plan a schedule" tab to add further preferences, like specific sections of courses, gen eds you'd like to fulfill, and how many credits you want.

Step 3: Let us do the work

Hit search and we'll show you the schedules that meet your requirements. It's that easy.
